Work Study Programme

The Work Study Programme of Anchor University is established principally to help students inculcate the concept and practice of work culture and to learn how to earn, learn, and leave legacies. Thus, every interested full-time regular undergraduate and post-graduate students of Anchor University are eligible to apply for the scheme provided the applicant is at least 18 years of age, in 200 level and above, and have a CGPA of 2.5 and above (out of a maximum of 4). Thus, no JUPEB, TOP-UP or part-time student is qualified to apply for this scheme.
